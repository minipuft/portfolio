## Resonant Feedback

When you interact with a system, it should echo back. Not with a static state change, but with a physics-based ripple that propagates through the grid.

### Key Principles

1.  **Breathing**: Idle states are never static.
2.  **Anticipation**: UI elements prepare for interaction before contact.
3.  **Resonance**: Actions have consequences that ripple outward.
